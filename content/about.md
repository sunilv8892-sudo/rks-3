title: About the Garden Tennis Club
---
The Garden Tennis Club, situated in Mysuru, is an institution of historical legacy and cultural significance. Its origins trace back to the colonial period when it was patronized by the British, and later stewarded by the distinguished Wodeyars of Mysuru.

In 1958, the club was formally registered under the Mysore Corporation, marking a defining step in its growth as an organized sporting institution.

The concept and early development of the club were guided by visionary leaders such as Pattabi Ramaiah, V. T. Raman, and L. A. Vasuki. Veeranarasimhaiah is remembered for exceptional and enduring contributions to the advancement of the club.

A major milestone in modern club history was the inauguration of the present building in 1995 by Shivarathri Deshikendra Mahaswamy, along with B. Siddaramaiah, then Finance Minister of Karnataka.

Today, the Garden Tennis Club continues to uphold discipline, sportsmanship, and community engagement while nurturing a long-standing passion for tennis.

## Mission
To promote social, cultural, and recreational activities alongside tennis and table tennis by providing well-equipped facilities, including study rooms and libraries; organizing entertainment programmes and outdoor and indoor sporting events from time to time; and creating an environment that supports the physical, intellectual, and moral development of members.

## Vision
To establish the Garden Tennis Club, Mysuru, as a distinguished centre of sporting excellence and community engagement, fostering holistic development through tennis, table tennis, and diverse cultural and recreational pursuits.

### Poojashree Venkatesha
- Former Indian professional tennis player
- Associated with Mysuru and Mandya, Karnataka
- Born: 27 July 1990
- Turned professional in 2006
- Competed primarily on the ITF Women's Circuit
- Silver Medal: 2008 Commonwealth Youth Games (Singles)
- ITF Titles: 5 Singles and 9 Doubles

### N. S. Ashrith
N. S. Ashrith is an accomplished tennis player associated with the Garden Tennis Club, Mysuru, who has represented Karnataka with distinction across district, state, and national competitions.

- 2014-2015: Winner, District Level Tennis Tournament; Runner-up, State Level U-16 Tournament; represented Karnataka at National Level U-16
- 2015-2016: First Place, State Level U-17 Tournament; represented Karnataka at National Level U-17 in Hyderabad, Telangana
- 2016-2017: Gold Medal at District and State events; selected for National Level for the third time, representing Karnataka in New Delhi
- 2017: Bronze Medal, Karnataka State Olympic Games (Men's Open Category) at KSLTA
- 2018-2019: Runner-up, VTU Inter-Collegiate Zonal Tournament at Belagavi; Runner-up, VTU Intra-Collegiate Zonal Tournament at Belagavi
- 2019-2020: Participant, VTU University Level Men's Tournament at Mandya

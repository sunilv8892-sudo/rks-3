The Garden Tennis Club, Mysuru, offers a comprehensive range of facilities designed to support sporting excellence and holistic member engagement.

- Well-maintained clay tennis courts among the finest in Mysuru
- Modern gymnasium for fitness and training support
- Dedicated spaces for indoor leisure: chess, table tennis, and card games
- Air-conditioned dining hall with projection facilities for social and formal events
- Well-stocked library and canteen amenities
- Fully functional meeting hall for member and committee use
- Serene natural environment for recreation and wellness
- Ongoing development and expansion with new facilities planned

### Key Achievements
- 2014-2015: Winner, District Level Tennis Tournament; Runner-up, State Level U-16; represented Karnataka at National Level U-16
- 2015-2016: First Place, State Level U-17; represented Karnataka at National Level U-17 in Hyderabad
- 2016-2017: Gold Medal at District and State events; selected for National Level (third time) in New Delhi
- 2017: Bronze Medal, Karnataka State Olympic Games (Men's Open Category) at KSLTA
- 2018-2019: Runner-up, VTU Inter-Collegiate Zonal and VTU Intra-Collegiate Zonal tournaments at Belagavi
- 2019-2020: Participant, VTU University Level Men's Tournament at Mandya

### Key Achievements
- Silver Medal: 2008 Commonwealth Youth Games (Singles)
- ITF Titles: 5 Singles and 9 Doubles
- Career-high rankings: Singles World No. 306, Doubles World No. 371

### Profile
- Born: 27 July 1990
- Turned professional in 2006
- Competed primarily on the ITF Women's Circuit

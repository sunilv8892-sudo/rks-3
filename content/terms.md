Membership, coaching access, and facility usage are governed by current club rules and committee decisions.

Please review all terms before booking courts, joining programmes, or participating in events.

## Membership
### Honorary Membership
Honorary Membership may be conferred upon individuals with distinction at national or state level in sports, social service, education, culture, or other fields of public importance. Elected representatives such as Municipal Council members and MLAs within club jurisdiction may also be considered.

The Managing Committee may invite such individuals during their term of office, subject to eligibility, good standing, and absence of criminal record.

Honorary Members:
- Are exempt from membership fees
- Do not possess voting rights
- Are not eligible to contest club elections

In Mysuru, the following dignitaries may be invited during their tenure:
- The First Citizen (Mayor)
- District Magistrate or Divisional Commissioner
- Deputy Commissioner (Land Revenue)
- Deputy Commissioner (Revenue)
- Deputy Commissioner (Excise)
- Commissioner of Police
- Commissioner of Corporation
- Commissioner, MUDA
- Chairman, MUDA
- Superintendent of Police
- Deputy Commissioner of Commercial Taxes
- Deputy Inspector General of Police
- Other senior officers or distinguished individuals, as determined by the Executive Committee

### Life Membership
The Managing Committee may grant Life Membership to existing members who have completed 25 years of continuous membership after attaining 18 years of age. Applicable subscriptions for Life Members are determined by the Committee.

The Board of Director has the authority to grant lifetime membership.

### Fee Structure
- Application Fee: Rs 500
- Entry Fee: Rs 50,000
- Entry Fee (Children of Existing Members): Rs 15,000

Monthly Subscription:
- Rs 375 for Tennis Playing Members
- Rs 150 for Non-Tennis Members
- Rs 150 for Life Member (Tennis)
- Rs 75 for Life Member (Non-Tennis)
- Rs 50 for non-seasonal tennis players

Additional Charges:
- Rs 50 per day for non-term players
- Guest Fee per day: Rs 150 for tennis players
- Guest Fee per day: Rs 50 for non-tennis players
- Relocation Fee: As determined by the Executive Committee
- Annual tennis fee for children of existing members: As fixed by the Executive Committee

### Termination of Membership
Membership may be terminated under the following circumstances:
- Voluntary resignation subject to clearance of all dues
- Failure to pay dues within the stipulated period
- Misconduct or actions detrimental to club interests

### Withdrawal from Membership
A member intending to withdraw must notify the Honorary Secretary in writing within the first week of the month in which subscription becomes due. Without such notice, the subscription for that period remains payable.
